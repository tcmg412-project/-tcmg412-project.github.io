About Me

Howdy, Welcome to my website. My name is Mike Barrenechea and I am the loudest and proudest member of the Fightin' Texas Aggie Class of 2024! I was born in Tampico, Tamaulipas on June 9, 2001 and moved to College Station, Texas on July 2015. Since then, I have followed my education through highschool, Blinn College  and Texas A&M University. I have been working at Lupe Tortilla  as a server and a bartender for two years and counting. I hope to share some of my personal life and goals in my website. When I am  not in school or at work I like to spend my time watching movies with my friends and read books.
